Case study · Inkhouse × Dialpad

Planning

I led this digital content plan from idea to execution. Once the video team sent over a storyboard, we brainstormed a full communications plan to promote Dialpad's first Super Bowl commercial — integrating PR and digital across social, influencer relations, and media. Objective: maximize reach and engagement before, during, and after launch.

Execution

We teased the spot with social posts to stir curiosity, paired with a press release. Influencers amplified it on social. Day-of and post-launch activations stretched the investment's reach as far as it could go. We tracked metrics weekly and made strategy adjustments as necessary.
